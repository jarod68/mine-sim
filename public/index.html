⛏ Mine Sim — How to play

Goal: drill blocks to reveal ore, draw a road network, and a fleet of shovels & haul trucks automatically digs, hauls and dumps ore at the crushers to earn credit. Everything runs authoritatively on the server.

Loop

drill ore → draw roads → shovels load trucks → trucks haul to a crusher → 💰 credit

Camera

  • Scroll to zoom · hold right-click to pan.

Mouse mode 🖱

  • Click a block to open it; Drill & Explore (or press X) reveals its composition (costs credit).
  • Click a vehicle for details; assign a truck to a shovel, toggle its debug path.
  • Drive manually with the arrow keys (light vehicles can move diagonally); haul trucks otherwise run on autopilot.
  • Move to a point: with a vehicle selected, press W (or the 🎯 Move to… button), then click a destination — it drives there via the shortest path (roads, or off-road where needed).
  • Click the parking pad to show resize handles — drag a side to grow/shrink it.

Road ✏️ / Eraser 🧽 modes

  • Drag to lay a one-way road (the flow follows your stroke); cross roads to make junctions.
  • Drawing toward a screen edge auto-pans the view so you can extend far.
  • Trucks navigate the network on the shortest legal route; the eraser removes road (parking stays).

Fleet 🛒

  • Buy assets to expand the fleet (haul trucks, shovels, a scout, a dozer, a grader) up to 150 vehicles.
  • Buy extra crushers (up to 5, $1,000,000 each) — then click the map to place each one.

Rich veins ⛰ (dozer)

  • Dark-brown triangulated zones are rich ore veins: a drill can't touch them and no road may cross them until they're prepared.
  • Buy a dozer (Liebherr PR776) and bring it within ~5 blocks of a vein — it auto-starts and sweeps the zone row by row (a violet line shows its path). Each block needs 10 passes, so the ore is uncovered gradually.
  • Once revealed, the very rich ore is mined like any drilled block — and roads may then be laid across it.

Road wear & grading 🛣

  • Roads wear out under traffic: after ~75 vehicle passes a cell degrades — it turns dark & hatched, and haul trucks crawl across it at half speed (only the worn stretch).
  • Buy a grader (Caterpillar 24): it roams the map on its own, driving over degraded cells to repair them. Several graders fan out to different areas; with nothing to fix they return to the parking.

Breakdowns 🛠

  • Shovels & haul trucks are reliable ~99% of the time, but one can break down at random: it freezes in place, smoking, and a popup alerts you.
  • A broken asset is flagged red in the top-left details panel and the Fleet list (⚠️).
  • To fix it, drive a light vehicle (LV) into a cell right next to it — a green repair ring fills over ~5 s, then it runs again.

Multiplayer

  • Create a game or join with its 5-letter code — everyone shares the same world live.
